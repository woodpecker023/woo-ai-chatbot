WooAI Widget Test Page

This is a test page to demonstrate the WooAI chat widget. The widget appears as a floating button in the bottom-right corner of the screen.

Click the chat button to open the widget and start a conversation. The widget supports streaming responses, product recommendations, and dark/light themes.
